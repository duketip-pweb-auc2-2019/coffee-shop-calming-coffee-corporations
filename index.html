Calming Coffee



~ About ~

Calming Coffee Co. was founded in 2019 by Nate Willis, revolving around the idea of having a relaxing environment for patrons to drink and ultimately, enjoy coffee. Nate Willis and his neighbors all had the idea of starting a coffee shop. Ella Whittney had a delightful coffee recipe that filled all of her family with joy (and energy). Star Sayarath had a dream job of being a waitress. Harrison Hyde was eager to manage the company. Nate Willis was dedicated to building the shop, making sure that the entire shop was powered with solar panels. He even made it so the temperature in the shop changes according to the temperature outside. The employees are dedicated to making sure that every customer that walks through the door enjoys their time here at the Calming Coffee Co. From 5 year olds getting hot chocolate, to 95 year olds enjoying breakfast time, everybody is welcome here to our Coffee shop! Our coffee is made with an original masterpiece of a recipe by Ella Whitney. We are open every day for 24 hours so anyone can enjoy coffee at anytime that they like to drink coffee! Our largest priority is meeting customers' needs and making sure that their experience here was the best it could possibly be!
